Our Story

The Art of Living Beautifully

Incorporated in 2013, Qilan International began with a singular vision: to bring world-class aesthetics to East Africa. What started as a curated import business has evolved into a powerhouse of local manufacturing.

We realized that true luxury requires control. By shifting to local production, we now craft bespoke cabinetry, orthopedic mattresses, and solid wood furniture right here in Rwanda—because your home should be as unique as your signature.
